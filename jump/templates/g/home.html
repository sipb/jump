g/

g/ (g.mit.edu) is a link shortening service, similar to bit.ly, made available to the MIT community (and anyone who clicks its links).

Want to add your own link? Visit g/manage.

If you're on campus, you can use g/ instead of the full URL. This will let you use very short links like g/about instead.
